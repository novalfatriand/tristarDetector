ABOUT US

Tristar Anugrah Perkasa

Established in 2022 and based in Indonesia, we specializes in the research, development, and manufacturing of ground water source detectors designed with ease of use to meet the needs of borehole drillers. Our jorney began in 2015 with a clear vision to create a simple, quick, and accurate ground water source detector product that is not only effective but also user-friendly, allowing borehole drillers to locate water sources with ease and precision. We are committed to delivering high quality product that enhances the success of drilling operations, ultimately contributing to sustainable water resource management.
